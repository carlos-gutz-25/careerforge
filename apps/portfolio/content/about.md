## Who I am

I am a Senior Software Engineer, bilingual in English and Spanish, with over a decade of
experience delivering scalable, maintainable full-stack applications. I work most naturally in
JavaScript, TypeScript, Node.js, and Vue, with a strong grounding in modern deployment
pipelines and cloud infrastructure. I care about clean, testable code and about shipping
production-ready features alongside cross-functional teams.

## Career arc

### Nintendo of America (2008 - 2018)

I started as an Automation Software Engineer, building automated testing tools in Python and
OpenCV that cut manual QA effort and reduced costs by roughly $161k per quarter. Over that
decade I progressed from Localization QA Tester to Project Manager, leading the technical
direction of cross-functional projects and managing teams of up to 40 people.

### Love's Travel Stops & Country Stores (2018 - 2021)

As an Application Developer, I built and maintained Node.js APIs for the Love's mobile app with
a strong focus on test-driven development and Agile practices. I developed Kafka producers and
consumers to integrate queue-management, retail POS, and payment-processing systems, built a
Firebase push-notification capability that lifted user engagement, and implemented the
high-volume Showers feature, which processed more than $150k in daily transactions.

### Heartland Payment Systems (2021 - 2025)

As a Senior Software Engineer in a PCI DSS environment, I led the migration of a data-intensive
application to Vue 3 with Pinia, improving the performance of complex sales-data
visualizations by over 30%. I cut API latency from more than 2 seconds to 40 milliseconds with
Redis caching that minimized Snowflake queries while keeping data fresh, maintained CI/CD
workflows in Azure DevOps, and architected a full-stack application with real-time pricing
across large-scale portfolios.

## How I work

I favor maintainable systems, clear technical reasoning, and long-term ownership. I am a
hands-on implementer who also brings architectural judgment and production-troubleshooting
experience, and I look for teams that value autonomy and durable, well-tested software.
